## 🎊 Quick Start - Add Your Photos NOW

### 3 Simple Steps:

**Step 1:** Copy your wedding photos into this folder
```
wedding-carousel/public/photos/
```

Name them: `wedding-1.jpg`, `wedding-2.jpg`, `wedding-3.jpg`, `wedding-4.jpg`

**Step 2:** Optional - Update the captions

Edit `src/App.jsx` and change the titles/subtitles to match your photos.

**Step 3:** The carousel is already running!

Visit: **http://localhost:5174**
